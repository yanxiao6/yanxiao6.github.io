About me

I am now a Research Fellow in SoC, National University of Singapore in the group of Prof. Jin Song Dong and Prof. David S. Rosenblum and under the supervision of Prof. Ivan Beschastnikh. I received my Ph.D degree in Computer Science from City University of Hong Kong, Master and Bachelor degree in Computer and Information from Hohai University. I have published some papers in refereed journals and conferences, such as ICSE, ToN, IST, APSEC, QRS, and so on. Thanks to the supervision of Prof. Hong Xu and Dr. George Trimponias, I started my research career at Huawei Noah’s Ark Lab. My research is within the domain of Software Engineering and Artificial Intelligence in application domains related to software bug localization, software code readability, software integration test, data mining, and big data analysis. My researches are undertaken in a broad spectrum of application domains, such as self-driving cars, deep neural network analysis, and hydrological data analysis. I now focus on: 1. trustworthiness evaluation for autonomous driving. 2. deep neural network repair for improving the accuracy of models. 3. adversarial test generation for deep learning systems.
